Everyone deserves the wedding of their dreams, whether it’s the elaborate and grand type of wedding or a small and private ceremony, the key thing is to have a wedding we desire. And rightly so, some persons fancy the grand and elaborate wedding ceremony. Lots of people want a unique venue, a customized wedding dress, giant cakes, and limousines.

For those who can afford elaborate weddings with all the accompanying hoopla and expense it’s a fine thing - and we all love to watch (perchance to dream…). It’s thought that most little girls dream of a big wedding with hundreds of attendees, but guys too, have their dreams. The main thing is that everyone is happy at the end of it.

Justin Timberlake And Jessica Biel - $6.5 million

The popular 7th Heaven star was married to her heartthrob, Justin Timberlake on the 9th of October, 2012. They had a colorful wedding which was held in Puglia, Italy. The wedding ceremony took place at the Borgo Egnazia resort. Jessica Biel wore a non-traditional pink ball gown made by the famous Italian designer Giambattista Valli.

The cost of the gown was reportedly around $100,000 alone. According to reports, the wedding cost was approximately $6.5 million. This includes the pink ball gown worn by the bride, the 63-room, 28-villa luxury hotel and other attendant costs of the wedding ceremony. Their wedding ceremony was truly a private and expensive one.

Kim Kardashian And Kanye West - $2.8 million

Kim Kardashian and Kanye West finally tied the knot in Florence, Italy. The popular reality TV star obviously doesn’t do things small or secretive. The public and the media have a lot to tell about her wedding. Her darling husband too has always been about show biz.

Their wedding ceremony was truly a grand one. Kim’s wedding dress was made by Givenchy, and her beautiful daughter’s flower girl dress matched mom’s. The popular Italian singer Andrea Bocelli sang at the wedding ceremony. The wedding venue was the popular Forte di Belvedere. The couple also spent time in Paris where they had a 600-person rehearsal dinner and a performance by Lana Del Rey.

Sean Parker And Alexandra Lenas - $10 million

The former Facebook president and co-founder of Napster had a lavish old-growth forest-themed wedding which reportedly cost $10 million. The venue preparation alone was done at the cost of $10 million. The couple’s wedding ceremony took place on a privately run area in the Big Sur area in a California Redwood Grove. After the wedding, they suffered backlash from the public as a local conversation group reported that their celebration had caused environmental damage.

The wedding guests were serenaded by the music of Sting. It was also reported that the guests drank bottles of wine from Napa, which cost as much as $500 per bottle.

Kim Kardashian And Chris Humphries - $10 million

Kim Kardashian sure loves lavish wedding ceremonies. Before she married Kanye West, she was briefly married to the basketball star Chris Humphries. Their wedding ceremony took place in 2011. It has been reported that the cost of the wedding ceremony was as much as $10 million.

The couple’s beautiful wedding cake cost $20,000 alone. Although the duo’s marriage lasted only 72 days, they most certainly had a wedding ceremony that had large media coverage. Kim Kardashian’s three wedding dresses were designed by the celebrity designer Vera Wang. She also had a large amount of jewelry to match her beautiful wedding dresses.

Michael Jordan And Yvette Prieto - $10 million

It was at the Episcopal Church of Bethesda-by-the-Sea in Florida, that the basketball star tied the knot with his love in 2013. This was followed by a reception at Bear’s Club in Jupiter, Florida. The price of the wedding ceremony was pegged at $10 million. US Weekly reported that the wedding guest list included the likes of Patrick Ewing, Spike Lee, and Tiger Woods, as well as other top athletes and celebrities.

Usher and Robin Thicke were among the musicians who entertained the wedding guests. The bride’s wedding dress was a French silk J'Aton Couture with Swarovski crystals. The love birds wedding cake was a gigantic white rum cake.

Lolita Osmanova And Gaspar Avdolyan - $10 million

The Russians are well known for their lavish and elaborate wedding celebrations. In the duo’s glamorous wedding ceremony no expense was spared. The wedding was held at the Dolby Theatre in Hollywood where the Oscar Award ceremony takes place. The wedding cake was a beautiful ten-tier golden cake.

According to the Russian newspaper Komsomolskaya Pravda, the cost of the wedding cake and flowers alone were said to have cost $500,000. The wedding guests were entertained by top-rated musicians like Lady Gaga and Jason Derulo. Top violinists, ballet dancers and traditional Russian dancers performed at the wedding reception.

Petra Ecclestone And James Stunt - $20 million

The stunning British heiress married her entrepreneur heartthrob James Stunt in 2011. The pair’s wedding ceremony cost approximately $20 million. Their wedding ceremony took place at the Odescalchi Castle near Lake Bracciano in Italy. The cost of the wedding venue reportedly cost the equivalent of $407,505 to hire.

The beautiful bride wore a traditional white strapless gown designed by Vera Wang. She wore a beautiful tiara and veil. The reception music included top-rated stars like Alicia Keys, The Black Eyed Peas and a DJ set by David Guetta. It was reported that the cost of the entertainment from those top music stars was to the tune of $3,417,791 million.

Angela Yeung And Huang Xiaoming - $31 million

The Chinese superstar who is best known by her stage name, Angelababy, tied the knot with fellow actor Huang Xiaoming in a lavish wedding ceremony that cost $31 million. The beautiful bride wore a custom Christian Dior gown that took almost five months to make. The bride’s ring alone was $1.5 million.

The grand ceremony was streamed online. Each wedding guest received a bagful of gifts and goodies that included a mobile phone. The wedding cake was a ten-foot cake shaped like a carousel which reportedly took a month to bake. The love birds also had an engagement photoshoot at the Eiffel Tower.

Prince William And Kate Middleton - $34 million

In 2011 the British crown prince married his college sweetheart, Kate Middleton. The royal wedding cost was $34 million and $32 million went into the security details of the wedding. The Royal bride wore an ivory and white satin gazar Alexander McQueen ball gown designed by Sarah Burton.

Her hair was styled by James Pryce of the Richard Ward Salon, and the Queen graciously loaned her the Cartier halo tiara from the Royal Jewellery collection. She rode to the wedding ceremony with her father in a vintage Rolls-Royce. During the evening reception that was hosted by Prince Charles, the Duchess of Sussex wore a beautiful satin gown also designed by Sarah Burton for Alexander McQueen.

Prince Harry And Meghan Markle - $45 million

The Royal Couple truly had the dream wedding of many. Their wedding ceremony cost $45 million. But most of the expense went to the security details of the Royal Wedding. The wedding took place at Westminster Abbey. After the wedding ceremony ended, the Duke and Duchess of Cambridge then proceeded to Buckingham Palace where the Queen hosted a stately lunch for 650 guests.

They later had an evening reception at Frogmore House which was hosted by Prince Charles. Only trusted friends and family members were invited. The Royal bride’s dress was designed by British designer Clare Waight Keller for Givenchy. The designer also designed the Duchess of Cambridge’s shoes.
