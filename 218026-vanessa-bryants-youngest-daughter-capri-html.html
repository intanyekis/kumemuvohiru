Vanessa Bryant's Youngest Daughter Capri Kobe Looks Adorable in a Gucci Swimsuit and Wet Curly Locks

Publish date: 2024-05-29

Vanessa and late Kobe Bryant's daughter Capri enjoyed some relaxing pool time with her family, all while looking cute and angelic in a chic Gucci swimsuit.

The youngest daughter of the late basketball legend Kobe Bryant and his wife Vanessa beat the heat as she enjoyed her time in the swimming pool.

Capri's mother took to Instagram to share an adorable snap of her where she donned a chic Gucci swimsuit with pink and green floral prints.

CAPRI IS KOBE'S MINI-ME

Her full head of curly black hair looked cute in the photo. "Koko Bean," Vanessa captioned the sweet snap. It was a nickname given to Capri when she was born in 2019.

Last month, the toddler celebrated her 1st birthday which was five months after her dad and sister Gianna tragically died.

Vanessa and Kobe Bryant's daughter Capri poses in a Gucci swimsuit on July 12, 2020. | Source: Instagram/VanessaBryant

Back in May, the baby girl reached an important milestone as she took her very first steps.

Vanessa took to Instagram share a heartwarming tribute dedicated to her youngest child with a throwback photo of herself and Kobe cradling their baby girl.

CAPRI'S MILESTONES

Back in May, the baby girl reached an important milestone as she took her very first steps. In a video shared by Vanessa, she stumbled across the toom for the first time before falling into her mother's arms.

Capri was named after her dad Kobe. When Capri was born last year, the proud father took to Instagram to announce the news. "We are beyond excited that our baby girl “Koko” has arrived!!" he wrote.

Earlier in May, Vanessa celebrated what would have been her late daughter Gianna's 14th birthday. In an emotional post, she shared how much she is loved and missed.

Gianna would also have graduated eight-grade in May. Vanessa marked the occasion by sharing a sweet photo of her diploma covered in white flowers.

Kobe and Gianna Bryant attend the WNBA All-Star Game 2019 on July 27, 2019 in Las Vegas, Nevada. | Source: Getty Images

Vanessa previously shared that Capri resembles her late sister Gianna and has her dad's eyes. Kobe and Gianna died on January 26 along with several others after their helicopter crashed in Calabasas, California.

Kobe and Vanessa would have celebrated their 19th wedding anniversary this year. Along with Gianna and Capri, the couple shared another daughter, Natalia.
