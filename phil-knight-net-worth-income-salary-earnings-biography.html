Phil Knight Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Publish date: 2024-08-20
Phillip Hampson Knight, better known as Phil Knight, is a well-known American philanthropist and businessman. Phil Knight is most known to the general public as a co-founder of the global company “Nike, Inc.” Knight and Bill Bowerman co-founded Nike in 1964, and it has since evolved to become one of the most well-known sports companies in the world. The business has employed approximately 44,000 individuals all around the globe since its inception. Nike owes a lot of its success to its marketing tactics and sponsorships, which helped company gain notoriety. Signing a deal with famous basketball star Michael Jordan, who wore “Nike” sneakers during games, was one of the company’s greatest boosters. The business began sponsoring a number of well-known teams and players as a consequence of the lucrative exposure, including Carl Lewis, Cristiano Ronaldo, Wayne Rooney, Neymar, and Ronaldo, to mention a few. In addition to “Nike,” Phil Knight is the chairman of “Laika,” an animation company that specialises in feature films and music videos. Phil Knight was inducted into the Naismith Memorial Basketball Hall of Fame in 2012 for his achievements.
