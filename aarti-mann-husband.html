Aarti Mann Husband Purvesh Mankad Is A Businessman

Publish date: 2024-06-29

Aarti Mann and Purvesh Mankad are happily married for a long time now. The couple is currently residing in Los Angeles, California.

At the time of their marriage, Purvesh was working as the Chief Financial Officer for the company named Braingem, which he founded in 1999. Mann, on the other hand, was making her breakthrough in the acting field with her role in The Big Bang Theory.

Their wedding ceremony was carried out in a traditional Christian manner. The ceremony was very close and private, with only a few guests invited. The couple has been together for a very long time and still going strong. They have a daughter named Nikita, who is fully grown and has started her own family.

Mann is active on Instagram under @aartimanncan where she has 19.5K followers. Her Instagram profile states that she is a writer, director, and actress. Mann is very protective when it comes to her personal life. But occasionally, she shares beautiful pictures of her daughter Nikita and her grandchildren.

Purvesh is not very active when it comes to using social media. Although, he does have an Instagram account under @pmankad where he occasionally shares a few precious moments of his life. He has twenty-five posts to date.
