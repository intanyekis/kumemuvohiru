Ethan Cutkosky’s Love Life

For longtime Shameless fans, there are a couple of reasons to think that Ethan Cutkosky is a lot older than he is. First off, Cutkosky starred in Shameless from 2011 until 2021 and since he has been in the public eye that long, it is easy to overestimate his age. On top of that, Cutkosky’s Shameless character was a lot more mature than most kids his age so he always seemed older. Despite the perception of him, Ethan Cutkosky still is only 22-years-old as of this writing according to Wikipedia.

In this day and age, it is extremely common for people in their early-20s to be dating around to have fun and figure out what they want in a partner. On top of that, it is noteworthy that most young stars seem to jump in and out of relationships even quicker than their non-famous peers. Despite all of that, whodatedwho.com reports that Ethan Cutkosky has been dating a young woman named Brielle Barbusca since 2015.

Of course, the only people who know what goes on in a relationship are the people involved. As a result, it would be foolish for anyone to make assumptions about Ethan Cutkosky and Brielle Barbusca’s relationship. That said, a lot of Cutkosky’s fans raised their eyebrows when Barbusca scrubbed every photo of her with Ethan from her social media. Still, there haven’t been any actual reports of a breakup and it is entirely possible that Barbusca simply decided to keep her relationship with Cutkosky more private.
