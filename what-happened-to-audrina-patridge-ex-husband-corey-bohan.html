What Happened To Audrina Patridge's Ex-Husband Corey Bohan After Their Divorce?

If you are a die-hard fan of The Hills, then you're definitely familiar with Audrina Patridge and her tumultuous marriage and subsequent divorce. The reality star met Corey Bohan in 2008. Audrina and Corey got married in 2016 and had a baby girl in the same year in June.

Corey Bohan did make an appearance on The Hills at some point. In fact, Audrina and Corey’s first date was featured on the show.

Unfortunately, their marriage didn’t work out. After going through so much back and forth, Audrina and Corey divorced in 2018.

Fans are wondering what Corey Bohan has been up to since his divorce and how he gets to still stay relevant in Hollywood.

Who Is Corey Bohan?

Corey Bohan is of Australian descent. He was born on January 15th, 1982 in Queensland, Australia. Corey’s childhood information is out of reach at the moment since he decided to keep that part of his life out of the public eye.

Besides that, Corey is a biker and an actor. According to Courier Mail, apart from being featured in reality television, Corey is a sports legend. Ever since Corey decided to move to Los Angeles in 2002, the decision opened major opportunities for him. Corey became the first Australian to ever win 3 X-game titles in a row. Back then, nobody could beat him in the BMX dirt bike contest.

Additionally, Corey also won the NORA Cup Awards for number one, not once but 5 times.

When Corey met and hit it off with Audrina Patridge on The Hills, he said that his focus from biking was almost diverted. “Hollywood is a wild, entertaining place. At first, it consumed me and took my focus off of my bike, but really, after experiencing the red carpet events, parties, and the people, in the end, it made my love for BMX stronger and my focus more powerful.”

In 2004, during an interview with The BMX Network, when Corey was just 24 years old, he was asked if he felt weird making a living riding a BMX bike. Corey’s response was, “Not so much weird but lucky I think. It’s pretty cool to look at your bike and see what it has done for me and the places I have taken it. It's small and the general public don’t understand how much fun it can be. I love the death stairs from businessmen while riding the street or something, if only they knew how much fun us bmxers are having. As Mr. T would say ‘I pity the fool’”

Corey’s love for biking pretty much started at a very early age in life for him and it seems like up until now, that love hasn’t ceased.

Corey Bohan And Audrina Patridge’s Relationship Timeline

Corey and Audrina Patridge are not the first celebrity couple to ever have an on-and-off relationship, and they won’t be the last either.

They started dating in 2008. Since the two were always having an on-and-off relationship, during their time apart, Audrina and Corey were free to date whomever they wanted.

In 2016, Corey and Audrina reunited and this time they got married in a beautiful wedding ceremony that was held in Hawaii. In Audrina's blog, she wrote, "We were thrilled to bring both our families and friends together to celebrate our lifelong commitment in one of our favorite vacation spots… Hawaii!"

Just nine months after the wedding, Audrina filed for a divorce. Along with it, she also filed for a temporary restraining order.

According to People, The Hills star, Audrina Patridge opened up by saying that she felt like her life was a mess after the divorce. "My life was a mess when the opportunity to do the show again came back. I was going through a divorce, and I was moving. There were times when I broke down on camera because I couldn’t hold it together. But my Hills family was so supportive and loving. They were there for me."

Also, Audrina added by equalizing divorce to mourning a death. She said, "Divorce is like mourning a death. You’re ending a chapter in your life. And when you have a child in the mix, it makes things harder. Then, to add in publicity and people judging you … makes it a million times worse.”

Eventually, Audrina and Corey's troubling divorce was finalized in December 2018. Audrina was granted full custody of their daughter Kirra. With that, Audrina had no choice but to prepare to be a single mom.

What Happened To Corey Bohan After The Divorce With Audrina Patridge?

After the divorce, Corey was seeking child support from his ex-wife Audrina Patridge. According to TMZ, in 2020, Corey claimed that the COVID-19 lockdown caused him to be jobless hence he needed child support.

TMZ and Blast were able to obtain the legal documents about this matter that was filed in Orange County Superior Court by Corey Bohan.

In the document, Corey said that he previously worked as a bartender, but he lost his job once the lockdown began. He added that since Audrina still makes a lot of money, their daughter Kirra should be able to live comfortably in both households. "I do not receive spousal support or child support from Petitioner, although she earns a significantly higher income than me. It is in the best interest of Kirra for the Court to establish a child support order as Petitioner earns a significantly higher income than Respondent." Corey said in the filing.

Fast-forward to 2021, according to Daily Mail, Corey, and Audrina's custody agreement was finalized. The judge allowed Corey to have an eight-hour supervised visitation with his daughter Kirra.

Additionally, according to TMZ, the court also ordered that when Kirra goes over to her dad during visitation hours, he has to handle all the bills to cater to her needs.
