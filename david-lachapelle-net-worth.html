David LaChapelle’s Net Worth

David LaChapelle, an American photographer and director, has a net worth of $5 million. He is known for his photography, which conveys social messages and references art history. His style has been described as being kitsch pop surrealism and slyly subversive. LaChapelle was born in Hartford, Connecticut, in March 1963, and studied at the School of Visual Arts in New York City. His first photograph was of his mother on a family vacation. LaChapelle was bullied in school for being gay and ran away from home at 15 to become a busboy at Studio 54.

LaChapelle has worked with numerous artists as a director, including The Dandy Warhols, Moby, Enrique Iglesias, Elton John, Mariah Carey, Christina Aguilera, Avril Lavigne, Jennifer Lopez, Whitney Houston, Blink 182, No Doubt, Britney Spears, Gwen Stefani, and Norah Jones. He has won awards for the short Krumped at the Sundance Film Festival and Aspen Shortfest in 2004. Several books of his works have been published.
