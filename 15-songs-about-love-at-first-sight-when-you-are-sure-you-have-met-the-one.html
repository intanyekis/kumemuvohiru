15 Songs About Love At First Sight

Publish date: 2024-08-28

Love is a beautiful feeling and when one is all caught up in such feeling, everything seems blissful. It is even more interesting and lovely when the person you love, loves you back in the same way and with equal energy.

Love songs are musical stories people in love or lovers tell to try to explain what they feel and how they feel it. The messages could be direct, to the person’s lover or it could be for public consumption.

Love songs have been around since time immemorial. It gets redefined as humans and their feelings evolve, however, it is a universal language.

In this article today, we will take a look at some love at first sight songs. Not just any love songs, those ones that depict what you may have felt when you saw someone for the first time and you ‘zing’ with the person.

We will share 15 of those beautiful love at first sight songs that try to explain how you must have felt at the instant.

Here Are 15 Songs About Falling In Love At First Sight

1. James Arthur – “Say You Won’t Let Go”

This song by James Arthur is about his encounter with a lady he met and instantly realized was his better half. They both met and sparked things off, dancing and drinking happily together, the first time they met.

But I wanna stay with you

Just say you won’t let go, just say you won’t let go
