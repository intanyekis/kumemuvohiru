Brian Robbins: Successful Career, Real Estate Ventures, and Net Worth

Brian Robbins is an American actor, film director, film producer, and television director with a net worth of $150 million dollars. He gained widespread fame for his work on the sitcom “Head of the Class” and went on to direct and produce several successful projects such as “Coach Carter,” “All That,” “One Tree Hill,” and “Sonny with a Chance.”

Robbins is also the founder of media and entertainment company AwesomenessTV, which has generated more than 17 billion views and has hundreds of millions of subscribers. In 2013, Dreamworks acquired majority control of the company for $33 million in cash, and the final valuation topped $100 million with earnouts.

In October 2018, Robbins became the new President of Nickelodeon with a salary believed to be around $6 million per year before bonuses. He has also invested in several real estate properties, including a $13.7 million mansion in Brentwood, a $12.4 million mansion in LA’s Hancock Park neighborhood, a Santa Barbara residence he bought from Ellen DeGeneres and Portia de Rossi for $6.98 million, and an epic Beverly Hills mansion he purchased for $16.8 million in February 2020.
