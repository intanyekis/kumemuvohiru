Publish date: 2024-05-25

Britney Spears is an undeniable pop music icon and you can celebrate her birthday with a look back at photos of her through the years, here.

Britney Spears became a worldwide phenomenon in the late 1990s and continued to release hit after hit through the 2000s. While her music career was unstoppable, Britney was dealing with a lot in her personal life. Mental health struggles lead to her conservatorship in 2008. The agreement left most of her personal decisions in the hands of her father, Jamie Spears.

She was released from the arrangement in late 2021 and has been happier than ever. In June 2022, she married her now-estranged husband, Sam Asghari. Despite their whirlwind romance, the two split in August 2023.
