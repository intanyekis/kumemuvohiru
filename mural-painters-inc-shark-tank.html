Emily Strauss – The Founder of Mural Painters Inc.

Emily Strauss is the visionary founder behind Mural Painters Inc., a thriving mural painting company. Her journey as an artistic entrepreneur is one marked by perseverance and a passion for creativity. Before starting Mural Painters Inc. in 2009, Emily owned a fashion business called Vandalize Apparel. However, her true calling was in the world of art, and she embarked on a new venture dedicated to bringing vibrant and awe-inspiring murals to life.

Emily’s path to success wasn’t without its challenges. She faced a late-stage Lyme disease diagnosis and had to overcome the obstacles that came along with leaving high school. Undeterred by setbacks, she earned her GED and pursued her passion for art by enrolling in an art college. It was during her time spent studying art that Emily began to notice the renaissance of murals in cities, sparking her entrepreneurial spirit.

With her boundless creativity and determination, Emily established Mural Painters Inc., creating a platform to showcase her artistic talent while providing a valuable service to clients. Through her venture, she has brought together a team of skilled mural painters who share her passion for creating captivating artwork. Emily’s unwavering dedication and commitment to transforming spaces with stunning murals have propelled Mural Painters Inc. to become the go-to company for exceptional mural painting services.
