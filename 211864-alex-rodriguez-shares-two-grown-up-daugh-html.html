Alex Rodriguez Shares Two Daughters with His Ex-wife Cynthia Scurtis Meet the Girls

Publish date: 2024-06-20

Alex Rodriguez’s two daughters with ex-wife Cynthia Scurtis are growing into lovely ladies. Natasha and Ella are also enjoying having Jennifer Lopez as their father’s fiancée.

The sisters are big fans of the “Let’s Get Loud” hitmaker, as reported by Hola. They enjoy Jennifer’s songs, which they would sing and dance.

Rodriguez and Lopez have a big family together, with the famous singer also mother to 11-year-old twins, Max and Emme. The singer shares her twins with ex-husband and fellow performer, Marc Anthony.

NATASHA AND ELLA’S TRAITS

Rodriguez has revealed some traits of Natasha, 15, and Ella, 11. He shares the two girls with ex-wife Scurtis, whom he divorced in 2008.

As reported by Closer Weekly, the baseball described Ella as more musically inclined. On the other hand, Natasha is an academic achiever.

Although she watches a lot of TV, Natasha manages to get good grades. Rodriguez also said that Ella is a funny girl.

JLO’S FAVORITE TEENAGER

Lopez has been a sweet soon-to-be stepmother to Natasha and Ella. In late 2019, she took to social media to greet Natasha on her 15th birthday.

The singer called Natasha “her favorite teenager,” as reported by Hola. She shared a photo of them giving each other a warm embrace.

Lopez and Rodriguez are now among the most famous couples in the world.

JOURNEY THROUGH LOVE

Lopez and Rodriguez announced their engagement in March 2019. The “Hustlers” star began dating the former New York Yankees baseball player in February 2017.

Prior to Rodriguez, Lopez had an on-off relationship with Casper Smart, her former backup dancer. They dated for almost five years after she and Anthony announced their split in 2011.

Lopez’s divorce from Anthony was finalized in June 2014. She was able to retain primary physical custody of their twins.

Lopez and Rodriguez are now among the most famous couples in the world. How they managed to blend their families well, having their own children from previous relationships, also inspired many.

His daughters have developed a special connection with the singer. Natasha and Ella, together with Emme, even performed a song during Lopez’s 50th birthday.

Lopez is not only blessed with beautiful twins and soon-to-be stepdaughters. She is also fortunate to have two supportive sisters, Leslie and Linda.

The singer’s sisters earlier spoke about the singer being down-to-earth despite her massive success. Lopez is regarded as the most influential Latin entertainer in the United States.
