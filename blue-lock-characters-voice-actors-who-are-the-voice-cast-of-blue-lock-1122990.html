Blue Lock continues to captivate viewers with its immersive storyline, which revolves around a great soccer program. The anime centers on Yoichi Isagi, a high school soccer player who accepts the Blue Lock challenge.  Kota wrote the series and published it by Kodansha. Tetsuaki Watanabe and Shunsuke Ishikawa directed their television series. An anime television series adaptation produced by Eight Bit premiered in October 2022.
