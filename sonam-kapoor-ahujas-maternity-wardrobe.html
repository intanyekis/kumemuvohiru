5 Looks To Bookmark From Sonam Kapoor Ahujas Maternity Wardrobe

Publish date: 2024-08-12

Maternity fashion needn’t be matronly—definitely not in the hands of Sonam Kapoor Ahuja. As one of the top fashionistas, she has spread her sartorial influence from Bollywood to the Cannes Film Festival. And now, the 36-year-old is ready to conquer a new frontier: motherhood. Ever since she shared the good news with an intimate post on Instagram, all eyes have been on the star, and she isn’t one to disappoint.

READ: Alia Bhatt Maternity Wear Line

Take notes from her wardrobe for the perfect look for every occasion on your calendar:

All-black bodysuit for lounging

Kapoor Ahuja let the world in on the big news with a heartfelt Instagram post of her lounging on the couch with her husband, Anand Ahuja. Despite the black-and-white portraits, her choice of a knitted bodysuit shines through with its understated ease.

Whimsical organza dress for brunch

For those leisurely mornings with your girls, you’ll want to bookmark Kapoor Ahuja’s floaty dress from Buna Studio with a fuss-free silhouette that offers maximum comfort and ease of movement. For the finishing touches, you’ll want to tuck a few fresh flowers into your hair for a summer-ready look.

Lace kaftan for dinner with the fam

If you’re looking to dress up without compromising on comfort, take note of the star’s sheer number from Fil De Vie for an elevated take on this everyday staple. With dark nails and defined eyes, the look will surely guarantee a few head-turns your way.

Ivory sari for birthday dinners

For those special occasions on your social calendar, you’ll want to step into an elegant ivory silk sari, like the one that Kapoor Ahuja chose for couturier and close friend Abu Jani’s birthday. While she opted for vintage draping inspired by ‘80s Bollywood, you can bring the look into the twenty-first century with a more contemporary drape.

Pantsuit for a day at work

If you are looking to indulge in the best of maternity fashion in the boardroom, make note of Kapoor Ahuja’s striking blazer and trouser set that offers special attention to detail—like the drawstring waistband that is mindful of the comfort required by a growing baby bump.
