Kristin Cavallari, Jay Cutlers Best Moments With Kids: Family Album

Publish date: 2024-06-03

Kristin Cavallari and Jay Cutler have nothing but love for their trio despite parting ways romantically in 2020.

Cavallari and Cutler announced in April 2020 that they were calling it quits after 10 years together in total.

Prior to their breakup, the Hills alum exclusively told Us Weekly that Cutler is “always” going to be the stricter parent.
