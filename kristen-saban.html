Nhoroondo yeKristen Saban 

Kristen Saban akaberekwa muna 1990 muAlabama, USA. Iye murongi wezviitiko uye anonyanya kuzivikanwa nekuve mwanasikana wekurera waNick Saban. Baba vake vanoonekwa semumwe wevarairidzi vakuru vekoreji uye vakabatsira kukwidziridza iyo University yeAlabama kuita nyeredzi.

Akanga ari mudzidzi weTuscaloosa Academy uye akanga ari chikamu chechikwata che cheerleading. Akapinda Yunivhesiti yeAlabama. Akashanda muchirongwa chenhabvu semubatsiri wemudzidzi uye mushandi wezviitiko paBryant-Denny Stadium, uko kunobata mitambo yenhabvu yepamba yeAlabama.

Anogona kuramba achikurukura nababa vake uye kutarisira zvimwe zviitiko zvemitambo kuburikidza nebasa. Akafukidza zvakare gorofu uye yevarume koreji basketball makwikwi seSEC.

Hupenyu hwehukama

Kristen Saban ainzi musikana waLane Kiffin. Zvisinei, havachisiri muhukama zvino. Muna 2015, nhau dzakabuda dzekuti murairidzi Kiffin aibatanidzwa naKristen murudo. Mashoko acho akanga ari musoro mukuru zvokuti Kiffin akanga asina chaaigona kuita kunze kwokurega semudzidzisi uye kurambana Layla, mudzimai wake.

Kunyangwe hazvo mhosva iyi yanga isina humbowo, nyaya yavaipomerwa yakakanganisa hupenyu nemabasa eavo vaibatanidzwa. Kristen akaroorwa naAdam Setas muna 2014 uye akaita mwana naye. Vave shamwari kubva pre-school.

Kristen naSetas vakaramba vachionana kunyangwe vaigara munzvimbo dzakasiyana. Gare gare, vakasangana zvakare muAlabama. Vakakudziridza manzwiro kune mumwe nemumwe apo Kristen anonzi akarambana nemukomana wake kuti ave naSetas. Mushure megore rekufambidzana, Setas akamupfimba pabhiriji rakatarisana neNashville. Mwedzi mishoma varoorana.

Rimwe gore asati aroorana naSetas, akapinda mubopoto rakazotungamirira kumatare. Chikumbiro ichi chakanzi Kristen nekuda kwekurova mumwe wehanzvadzi dzake dzehuroyi. Imwe hanzvadzi yake yehun’anga inonzi yainyora paFacebook nyaya yavaive nayo izvo zvakazoita kuti imwe hanzvadzi iyi vaonesane nayo muimba yavo yekurara. Gare gare, dare rakaramba mhosva yacho nokuti Kristen aingozvidzivirira.

Kristen Saban Akatorwa Here?

Saban ndiye mwanasikana akarerwa naNick Saban naTerry Saban, mudzimai wake. Achitaura nezvemurairidzi Nick, Athlon Sports akataura kuti vaviri ava vakatora Kristen nemukoma wake Nicholas. Pashure pokunge varoorana muna 1971 vava nemakore 21, vaviri vacho vakavatora.

Kristen Twitter Controversy

Mushure mechirevo chekunonoka kweCollege Soccer Playoff National Championship nekuda kweOhio State's COVID kesi, Kristen, murairidzi mukuru weAlabama mwana waNick Saban, akanyora tweet ine gakava.

Iye tweeted kuti aishoropodza Ohio State's ikozvino COVID nyaya. Akataura kuti vaive "BS" uye kuti maBuckeyes aiyedza kupa Justin Fields nguva yekupora kubva mukukuvadzwa kwaakaita achipikisa Clemson.

Kristen akanyora mune yakadzimwa tweet, "Kana usina chivimbo chekutamba, saka TAI IT." "Ndinofonera BS nezvenyaya dzeCOVID. Ivo vanongonetseka nezve quarterback yavo uye vanoda kuti agone kupora. Iwe unogona kugara uine backup painoda. Hatina kumbosendeka mwaka wasara kuti tifadze Waddle. CHISARAI." Kristen aiziva kuti tweet yake yaisafanira.

Akadzima iyo tweet uye kwenguva pfupi akavhara yake Twitter account. Mushure mechinguva, Kristen akadzoreredza yake Twitter account. Kristen akatumirawo ruregerero kuOhio State nezve tweet yake yakabviswa. Anodemba matauriro aakaita pamaBuckeyes. Kristen akakumbira ruregerero, achiti tweet yaakatumira yakanga isiriyo. “Ndakakanganisa zvikuru, uye ndinokumbira ruregerero nomwoyo wose. Ndakaita hasha ndikarega hasha dzichindikurira. Ndinovimba kuti munogona kundipa ruregerero rwenyu”.
