Monica Responds To Ray J's Request For A Joint Tour With Brandy

Publish date: 2024-08-31

Monica shut down all rumors regarding a tour with Brandy in a lengthy response to Ray J, who’s been spearheading the campaign for said tour.

On Wednesday (Mar. 20), the “Angel Of Mine” singer took to Instagram addressing Ray directly after he appeared on The Breakfast Club and discussed the potential co-headlining endeavor.

“I’ve not received any contracts or calls about said tour,” Monica wrote. “@rayj i’m kindly asking you to stop speaking on me in public! A private conversation would be both necessary and respectful!”

She reiterated prior sentiments on Brandy, noting, “Brandy is a LEGEND! She’s one of one with an extensive catalog that I deeply respect & a voice sent from heaven!” However, for Monica, that doesn’t negate the fact that a conversation between herself and Brandy should happen before Ray started his press run.

“The conversation being had without she & I is beginning to muddy the waters severely!” wrote the mother of three. When Ray spoke with The Breakfast Club, he stated that Monica should open, and that definitely rubbed her the wrong way as well.

The “So Gone” singer concluded, “She and I are both consummate professionals that share a massive recording as well as an entire era! […] Neither should open, we should give someone else that opportunity and co-headline a massive shared stage if this is to ever happen! Any further convo should be private!”

Ray first mentioned the idea of a tour last week. He commented on Monica’s Instagram Live and wrote, “BRANDY/MONICA TOUR – PLEASE LETS MAKE HISTORY, ONE RUN FOR THE FANS. 100M plus for you both.”

Brandy has not addressed Monica or Ray J publicly on this matter.
