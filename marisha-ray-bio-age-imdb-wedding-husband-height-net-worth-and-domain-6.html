Marisha Ray Career

Marisha produced a Batgirl fan series in the year 2012 called Batgirl: Spoiled, in which she played the titular character, in 2015, Marisha began co-starring in the web series Critical Role, on which Marisha joins fellow voice actors in playing Dungeons & Dragons Fifth Edition. Marisha’s character for the first campaign was Keyleth, a half-elf druid. As a part of getting into her character, Marisha compiled a playlist representing Keyleth’s journey.

Her character for the second campaign is Beauregard, a human monk, Marisha was involved in many of Geek & Sundry’s other productions, including co-hosting Key Questions, acting in Sagas of Sundry, as well as producing Signal Boost! Marisha announced her appointment to the post of creative director for Geek & Sundry on July 28, 2017.

However, Marisha stepped down from the position in June 2018, when Marisha became the creative director for Critical Role Productions.
