Here's What Love Is Blind's SK Alagbada And Raven Ross Have Said About Their Relationship And Ultima

Publish date: 2024-09-05

Love is Blind season three couple Sikiru ‘SK’ Alagbada and Raven Ross take the cake for the most dramatic breakup in the show’s history. When Alagbada responded “I don’t” at the season finale, fans assumed that the duo’s relationship had reached its culmination. However, barely a year after their failed nuptials, Alagbada and Ross attended season three’s reunion special, where they announced that they’d rekindled their romance.

Unfortunately, the duo’s reunion was unexpectedly cut short when barely weeks after filming the reunion special, SK got caught up in an embarrassing cheating scandal. With SK still facing backlash for allegedly cheating on his former fiancé, we take a look what the two have said about their relationship and rather dramatic split.

RELATED: How Love Is Blind’s Barnett And Amber Make Their Marriage Last

8 SK Alagbada And Raven Ross’ Love Is Blind Nuptials Didn’t Go As Planned

Although her relationship with SK Alagbada was nowhere near perfect, Raven Ross walked down the aisle on Love is Blind season three’s finale episode prepared to get married. The 29-year-old pilates instructor was, therefore, caught completely off guard when Alagbada responded “I don’t” to their vows.

"I definitely was unprepared. We had gone through this whole experience,” she would later tellPeople. “I was definitely on cloud nine after our build-up of having a great relationship, so I was a little shocked."

7 Why Did SK Alagbada Reject Raven Ross At The Altar?

As it turns out, SK Alagbada had a pretty good reason for rejecting Raven Ross at the altar.

“It was like merging two different people from two different continents trying to make this work,” he told US Weekly in November. “And I just felt like we still had a lot of unresolved differences that adding a marriage on top of that still would not help us resolve.”

6 SK Alagbada And Raven Ross Went Their Separate Ways After Love Is Blind Wrapped

Following their failed nuptials, SK Alagbada and Raven Ross’ love lives reverted to their pre-Love is Blind status.

"When Raven and I left the altar, I went about living my life as a single guy, and she went about living her life as a single woman," SK recently explained to Life & Style. “It took a while for Raven and I to even consider or attempt to start dating again.”

5 SK Alagbada Reached Out To Raven Ross Shortly After Their Failed Nuptials

However, SK Alagbada soon reached out to Raven, hoping to smooth things over and rekindle their romance.

“He reached out and said, ‘I’m sorry. I hope we can rebuild things and just keep going with our connection in the same way,’” Raven revealed to Variety. “I was like, ‘Yeah, you have to say sorry, sir!’ Luckily, after a few days, we were in the same place mentally of, ‘OK, I don’t want to lose this person. So how do we move forward?’”

4 SK Alagbada And Raven Ross Even Got Acquainted With Each Other’s Families

Raven Ross and SK Alagbada made some major strides in their relationship after their reunion. The duo even got acquainted with each other’s families.

“We’ve visited my family. I’m originally from New Orleans, so we went down to New Orleans,” Raven admitted to Variety in early November. “My family has come to Dallas. We’ve stayed at my house. We’ve done it all. We visited his family a lot in Austin. We’ve all made those connections, and he feels a lot better about it."

3 SK Alagbada And Raven Ross’ Reunion Didn’t Last Very Long

However, just as their post-Love is Blind romance was starting to gain traction, reports surfaced on TikTok that Alagbada had cheated on Raven on multiple occasions.

Among those embroiled in the cheating scandal is influencer Hannah Beth Style, who claimed that Alagbada took her on a romantic vacation to Europe, despite being engaged to Raven Ross.

2 SK Alagbada’s Cheating Scandal Ended His Relationship With Raven Ross

Amid fan backlash, SK Alagbada and Raven Ross announced that they’d decided to end their relationship via a joint social media statement. The announcement, which was posted to their respective Instagram stories, also seemed to imply that the duo was pursuing legal action against Alagbada’s accusers.

"We are saddened to announce that we have decided to go our separate ways," the statement read. "Due to ongoing legal proceedings surrounding these allegations, we cannot provide additional details and ask that you please respect our privacy during this hard time.”

1 SK Alagbada Denies Having A Relationship With TikToker Hannah Beth Style

SK Alagbada has denied cheating on Raven Ross, claiming that his accusers were motivated by financial gain. In his interview with Life & Style, the 34-year-old data engineer also fervently denied ever having been in a relationship with social media influencer Hannah Beth Style.

"All the allegations about me cheating are false," he said. "At no point did we consider each other boyfriend and girlfriend. She is not my ex...the situation between Hannah, and I was never formal."
