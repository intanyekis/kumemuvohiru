Victoria Caputo is sharing the Christmas spirit with her fans. The daughter of "Long Island Medium" star, Theresa Caputo, channeled warm festive vibes in a snapshot with her fiancé, Michael Mastrandrea.

The TV star gave off Christmas vibes in a new snapshot that features her posing alongside, Michael Mastrandrea, her husband-to-be.

The lovebirds glowed as they smiled for the camera in a Christmas-ready room. The couple got engaged in February 2019 after being together for a little over a year.

THERESA AND MICHAEL ARE CLOSE

When her precious daughter got engaged to her love, Theresa was happy for her. She showed her love for the couple through a loving Instagram post on February 16.

The reality star seems to be close to her daughter's fiancé. The duo occasionally appears in each other's Instagram, and when they do, they have nothing but kind words for one another. Victoria even took to Twitter to express her affinity for tattoos back in 2012. "Tattoos are addicting #iwantanotherone," she wrote.

THE PERFECT COUPLE

READY TO TIE THE KNOT

Victoria gave her fans a hint about when she is walking down the aisle in an Instagram post that she shared at the end of September. In the same post, she also revealed that she is trying her best to focus on her fitness.

The wedding planning began ever since the couple was engaged. Victoria even said "yes' to a dress from Kleinfeld's on TLC's hit show "Say Yes to the Dress."

THERESA STILL LOVES HER EX
